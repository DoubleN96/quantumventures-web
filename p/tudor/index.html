04 · The model (Ángel Aparicio)

Free weekly class → paid community

The proven mechanism: a free LIVE class every week (fixed day and time) that genuinely teaches and, at the end, invites people into the paid community. Evergreen, with real scarcity and a price that rises in tiers = perceived value that keeps growing.

🔁 Weekly mini-launch

Each class is a recurring sales opportunity, not a single launch once a year.

🔒 Real scarcity

Access in windows + locked founding price + bonuses that expire. "Get in before it goes up."

📈 Scaled price

The price only rises in tiers as the community grows. Whoever joins earlier pays less forever.
