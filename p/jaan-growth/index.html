02 · Two engines, one system

How we accelerate the growth

Two demand engines running in parallel, both feeding the same pipeline and the same market intelligence. One builds pull (desire); the other builds push (systematic acquisition).

Engine 1 · Pull

🎤 Influencer venture builder

We "buy influence": a network of Bali creators that places Jaan and amplifies it.

  • Creators get Jaan into venues (B2B doors) and onto feeds (B2C demand).
  • "Place first, then amplify" — bottles in the venue, then the content that drives footfall.
  • Raises Jaan's perceived value → premium pricing power.
  • Each creator carries a referral code → fully tracked.

Engine 2 · Push

📧 Mass personalised outreach + forms

Personalised emails to every restaurant, café and hotel we've mapped — each with a form inside.

  • AI personalises each message per venue (type, area, name).
  • A form inside the email captures their current water: brand, volume, supplier, price.
  • That data feeds a realistic market study — real demand, real prices.
  • We respond with a personalised offer per venue. No generic pitch.
